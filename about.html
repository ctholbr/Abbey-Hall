About Us

Through the leadership of company founder Laurie Holbrook, ASID, Allied Member, IIDA, Abbey Hall has embracing promising scientific research on consumer behavior, human healing and productive spaces and incorporated it into our sensory interior design process. Through this process, Abbey Hall creates the ideal environment to produce positive outcomes in people.

Without sacrificing function or form, our designers enhance the architectural elements of space, incorporating color and design with the feel of a fine home and the plush comfort of antiques and quality fabrics.
