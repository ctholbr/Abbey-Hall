Taste

Although taste is not commonly thought of when decorating, the sense of taste makes a space feel more like home. Most of us associate home with food. Food, and taste, can also make a space feel more inviting and welcoming. Waiting rooms that have fresh natural food offerings such as pitchers of water with lemons or cucumber slices and offerings of fresh fruit can be a way to support health and relaxation. Additionally, many foods are linked with rapid healing and creative thinking.
